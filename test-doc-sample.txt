
PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is made and entered into as of July 15, 2025 (the "Effective Date"), by and between:

BlueEarth Capital LLC, a Delaware limited liability company with its principal place of business at 123 Finance Street, New York, NY 10001 ("Client")

AND

Acme Consulting Group, Inc., a California corporation with its principal place of business at 456 Advisory Lane, San Francisco, CA 94105 ("Consultant")

RECITALS:

WHEREAS, Client desires to engage Consultant to provide certain professional services; and
WHEREAS, Consultant desires to provide such services to Client;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements hereinafter set forth, the parties agree as follows:

1. SERVICES
1.1 Services. Consultant will provide to Client the services ("Services") described in one or more statements of work signed by both parties (each, a "Statement of Work" or "SOW").
1.2 Statements of Work. Each SOW will include, at a minimum: (a) a description of the Services to be performed; (b) the timeline for performance; (c) the fees for the Services; and (d) any other relevant terms and conditions.

2. TERM AND TERMINATION
2.1 Term. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue until December 31, 2027, unless earlier terminated as provided herein (the "Term").
2.2 Termination for Convenience. Either party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice to the other party.

3. COMPENSATION
3.1 Fees. Client shall pay Consultant the fees set forth in each SOW.
3.2 Expenses. Client shall reimburse Consultant for reasonable and documented expenses incurred in connection with the Services, provided such expenses are approved by Client in advance.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
4.1 Confidential Information. Each party acknowledges that it may have access to certain confidential and proprietary information of the other party.
4.2 Non-Disclosure. Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's confidential information and not to disclose such information to any third party.

5. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York, without giving effect to any choice of law principles.
